About Us



TN was formed on the 10 May 2018. The first independent community print media, reporting different stories in rural areas. For almost 3 years, the Turnstar Newspaper has been serving the community as the source of people living in rural areas when it comes to the news that matters. Since starting as a small family run newspaper, we have grown with the city, telling the stories of the capital, its people and their interests.


The management of TN consists of Chief News Reporter (CNR), Marketing Manager (MM), Operations Manager and Finance Manager (FM).






Our Goal



To educate and inform the communities with commitment to telling the stories of the people of Rural areas, providing the best coverage of the public service and politics in the nation and keeping you informed about your community.
